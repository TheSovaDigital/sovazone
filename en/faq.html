How do I put the username onto my own account?

Username transfer is a separate paid service. The price is discussed individually for each handle.

Trying to free the name on one account and place it on another by yourself is not recommended because the risk of losing the username is high.

How much does username transfer cost?

The price depends on the specific handle, its rarity, market interest, and the technical complexity of the transfer.

A rough range may start around 500 USD and reach 3000 USD or more for stronger and more complex usernames.

Why can’t I transfer the username by myself?

On Instagram, a username may not become safely transferable immediately after certain actions, and trying to move it without the right logic can lead to a loss.

Even if you wait through a hold period, attractive names can still be taken by other users or automated tools.

Are there other ways to change the username by yourself?

Technically, a user can try to free a handle and take it on another account independently, but such actions are always taken at their own risk.

If the username is attractive to the market, the risk of interception remains high.

What are the chances of losing a username during self-transfer?

Short usernames, especially rare and desirable ones, usually fall into a higher-risk category.

The shorter and more valuable the name, the higher the chance of losing it without guided transfer logic.
