Where to place a listing

To publish a username sale listing, use the SovaUsername.

Listings are published according to the rules shown in the pinned post and in the example post from the channel administration.

What to do before publishing

Before publishing, carefully review the channel rules, formatting requirements, and the example of a correct listing.

This is a required condition for publication. The listing must fully match the platform rules.

Moderation and removal

Listings that break the rules can be removed by the administration without additional approval.

If you want to publish a username correctly and avoid extra revisions, follow the pinned post and the template published by the channel administration.
