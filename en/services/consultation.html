Consulting

  • How to protect the account from repeated problems
  • How can I move a username between accounts?

  • How should I act in a difficult situation?

How consulting works

  • You describe your situation
  • We evaluate possible solution paths
  • We explain risks and likely outcomes
  • We give recommendations for the next steps
  • If the situation requires actual execution
  • The work format is discussed separately
  • Timing and scope are agreed
  • After that, the next steps are decided

All consultations are free until the situation requires specific actions, checks, or actual work.
