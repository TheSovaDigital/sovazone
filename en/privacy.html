Privacy Policy

This page explains how contact-related information is handled when users communicate with SovaZone.

The project processes only the information needed to answer requests, discuss deals, provide services, and maintain communication. The exact scope depends on the nature of the request.

Users should avoid sending unnecessary confidential data. If a case requires additional details, they are discussed in context and only to the extent needed for the request.

For questions about data handling, use the contact page and the main Telegram route.
