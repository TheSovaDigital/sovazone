What information can be processed

When visiting the site, moving through its pages, using contact forms, reaching out through Telegram, or completing a deal, technical and contact information may be processed if it is voluntarily provided by the user or automatically recorded by analytics tools.

This may include a name, username, profile links, message text, device-related technical data, IP address, cookies, on-site actions, and other parameters needed for analytics, security, and request handling.

Why the information is used

Information is used to answer requests, discuss deals, support users, analyze traffic, improve page structure, understand user behavior, and improve the quality of the service.

The site may use statistics tools, analytics, visit logs, as well as tools such as session replay, click maps, and other behavior-analysis services.

International nature of the audience

The project is designed for users from different countries. By using the site, the user understands and accepts that data may be processed to the extent necessary for website operation, analytics, communication, and deal support.

If additional jurisdiction-specific requirements apply to a user, they may choose not to use the site, limit cookies, or stop interacting with the project.

Data transfer and security

Information is not used outside the purposes of the project, except where required by law, technical necessity, analytics services, or the execution of actions explicitly agreed with the user.

At the same time, absolute technical security of any internet-based communication cannot be guaranteed, and the user understands the usual risks associated with using the Internet.
