How to unblock an Instagram account

If an Instagram account has been disabled, one of the core lines of action is to use support forms, send clean appeals, and keep enough time between attempts. This guide is built as a practical instruction: what to send, how to write, and what kind of photos to prepare if Instagram replies with a document request.

Important: forms can work inconsistently. Some of them are unavailable from time to time, others may not open for every user. If a specific form fails, it is often better to move on to the next one rather than getting stuck on a broken page.

Recovery forms

The exact set of active forms can change over time, so the useful principle is not “send everything at once”, but “work through the available options in an orderly sequence”.

How to submit forms correctly

  • Send them one by one rather than all at once.
  • Do not send more often than once every 3 days, regardless of the specific form.
  • Write appeals in English.
  • It is usually better to do this from the phone that had already been used with the account.
  • If one form does not work, skip it and continue with the next available route.
  • If the form allows a choice between a personal account and a company, alternating the format can sometimes help.

If company-related data is being sent, it often makes sense to use documents in English that confirm activity, sales, or purchases.

What to do through the app in parallel

At the same time, the issue can also be reported through the app itself. This should also be done no more than once every 3 days.

  • A second account on the same device can be used for this.
  • Path in the app: Menu → Settings → Help → Report a problem.
  • If shake-to-report is enabled, you can simply shake the phone and send the message.
  • The explanation should be short, clear, and polite.

Example appeal text

My main account @username was disabled on DATE for the reason shown during the block. We sincerely apologize if we unintentionally caused any issue. Please review the account again.

@username
Email
Phone number

It is better to adapt the text to your own case. The appeal should not ask emotional questions such as “why exactly did you block me?” — the goal is to look clear, respectful, and review-ready.

What usually happens next

  • You may receive no answer to the forms at all.
  • No one is required to enter into correspondence or explain the details of the block.
  • If the case is reviewed, the process can take several weeks.
  • Sometimes the result comes after 3–5 weeks, with an email stating that the block was a mistake.

How to take photos if they ask for them

If the reply requests a photo with a document or a code, it is important to follow the instructions as precisely as possible.

Passport photo example

An international passport is often the cleaner option. Hold the document with both hands so the fingers are visible and the text is not covered. The head should remain fully visible.

Document photo example

The document should be visible in the frame, and both the hands and head should be shown fully without cropping.

Code-on-paper photo example

Use a white sheet and black printed letters. The head and hands should again be fully visible in the frame.

Additional route — direct letter

As an additional parallel path, some users also try sending an official written letter directly to the company. This does not guarantee a result, but for some people it becomes one more formal attempt when normal forms are unstable.
