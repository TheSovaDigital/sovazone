Experience and reputation

Work online has been ongoing since 2009. Over that time, different projects, services, and deals connected with digital assets, databases, accounts, and online directions have been completed.

Within this niche there is experience on specialized platforms, forums, and marketplaces where feedback, seller status, and reputation have been built up over time.

Verifiability

Some older reviews and profiles can still be found through search engines using contacts, registration dates, and related traces of activity on niche platforms.

You can also check domain age through public whois services and review feedback published in a dedicated Telegram channel: SovaOtzyv.

Deal format

For larger sums, an in-person meeting format is possible if it is agreed in advance by both sides.

If needed, additional ways to confirm the deal can be discussed when they fit the specific situation.

Important to understand

Because of the niche and the product type, some clients prefer to remain anonymous, so not every user leaves a public review. This is a normal practice in the area of unique usernames and digital assets.

Guarantees within the project are based on experience, reputation, verifiable activity history, transparent discussion of terms before the deal, and the ability to agree on the transfer process in advance.
